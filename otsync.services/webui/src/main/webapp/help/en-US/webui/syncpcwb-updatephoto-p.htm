Updating Your Photo

To update your photo:

  1. In the top, right pane, click your name, and then click Profile.

  2. Do one of the following:

    • Click Browse, and select the photo you want to use.

      The photo must be in .png, .gif, or .jpg format. It will be resized and cropped if necessary to fit the available space in the display.

    • Click Use Default to use the generic profile image.

  3. Click Save Changes.

 

Note:
If you use OpenText Content Server Pulse, updating your OpenText Tempo Box photo also updates your Pulse Profile photo.
