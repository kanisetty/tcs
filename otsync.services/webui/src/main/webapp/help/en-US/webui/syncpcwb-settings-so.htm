OpenText Tempo Box: Web Client User Help

   

Configuring Settings

In the top, right pane, clicking your name reveals a menu where you can do the following:

  • Update the photo that other users see when viewing folders that are shared with you.

  • Configure OpenText Tempo Box, Content Server Edition to notify you by email whenever someone invites you to share a folder, or whenever a shared folder is updated. Your administrator must enable email notifications for you to receive them.

  • View version information.

  • Sign out of the Web client.
