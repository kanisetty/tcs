Updating the Status of a Task

To update the status of a task:

  1. Select the folder that has an active task assigned to you or click the task notification icon .

     

    Tip:
    Folders with active tasks will show a task icon . For example, . When the status of all of the tasks is set to Completed or Cancelled, the task icon disappears.

     

  2. Click TASKS in the right pane. The list of all available tasks displays.

  3. Locate the task assigned to you and change the status from Pending to one of the other settings. If a task is not assigned, you can update the task status. Updating the status of an unassigned task does not assign the task to you. You cannot update the status of the tasks assigned to other users.
