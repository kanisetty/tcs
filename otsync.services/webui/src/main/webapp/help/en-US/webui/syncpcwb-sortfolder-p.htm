Sorting the Folder View

To sort the folder view:

  1. Click a column title to sort the content by name, last modified, or size.

  2. Optional To reverse the sort order, click the column title again.

 

Note:
If you are in thumbnail view, point to Sort By in the top right corner to click a sorting method. For more information on the thumbnail view, see Switching to Thumbnail View.
